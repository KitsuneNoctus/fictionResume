Coran - Head Engineer of the Castle of Lions & Altean Royal Advisor


Coran is the head and last remaining engineer of the Castle of Lions, the Main ship of the Altean Royal family, that house's the legendary lions of Voltron. He also serves as the emotional support for the paladins of Voltron, and acts as the aid of Princess Allura, the last remaining member of the Altean royalty.
He well versed in the studies of numerous alien cultures. Most of his time is spent on the ship providing the paladins with vital knowledge for thier mission, though not always accurate due to the loss of time being frozen for 10,000 years.
